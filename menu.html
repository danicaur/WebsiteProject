Dani's Special Oatmeal

Ingredients: ½ cup of oats 1 tbsp of flax meal 1 cup of almond milk 1 tsp of vanilla extract 1/4 cup of blueberries ½ banana 1 tsp of cinnamon Dash of salt Lots of love 💕

Instructions: Place the oats and milk in a pot on low heat, slowly add the flax seeds,and vanilla extract. Once the oats are cooked add the blueberries and cinnamon. Mix and divide into two bowls. Slice half a banana as a final topping and enjoy!

Cinnamon Swirl French Toast

I had been thinking about cinnamon swirl French toast these past weeks and woke up this morning on a breakfast 🥞 mission.

Mix - 2 eggs - ½ cup of almond milk - 1 drop of sweetener - Ground cinnamon (one of my favorite spices) - 1 tsp of vanilla extract Soak up cinnamon swirl toast and then transfer to a pan! Voilá
